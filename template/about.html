Why Student Grievance Redressal Cell

A Student Grievance Redressal Cell serves as a dedicated platform for addressing and resolving concerns and complaints raised by students. It ensures that grievances related to academic, administrative, or personal issues are handled in a timely and fair manner, fostering a supportive and transparent environment within educational institutions.
